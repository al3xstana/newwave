Cruise

Experience the allure of Marathonisi Island like never before with our exclusive Marathon Island cruise at New Wave. Departing from Zakynthos, Greece, our luxurious boat journey takes you on a captivating voyage to this hidden gem of the Ionian Sea.
The Keri Caves offer a mesmerizing journey into the island’s geological wonders.
Dive into the crystal-clear waters for a refreshing swim or snorkel among colorful marine life, immersing yourself in the serene beauty of this coastal paradise.

We start our trip from the port of Agios Sostis

The Island is nestled in the sparkling waters of the Ionian Sea off the coast of Zakynthos, Greece, is a pristine paradise awaiting discovery. Also known as Turtle Island due to its significance as a nesting site for loggerhead sea turtles.

The Caves are a mesmerizing labyrinth of natural wonders waiting to be explored. Located along the rugged coastline of the island’s southwestern tip, these remarkable sea caves captivate visitors with their stunning beauty and geological formations.

Located on the rugged western coast of Zakynthos, Greece, is a geological marvel that captivates visitors with its unique rock formations and breathtaking views. These towering sea stacks rise majestically from the crystalline waters of the Ionian Sea, sculpted over millennia by the relentless forces of wind and waves. Here we will relax and watch the famous sunset of zakynthos.

We will have the opportunity to see its golden shores but also to engage in turtle spotting.

Zakynthos is renowned for its population of loggerhead sea turtles, which nest along the island’s coastline. With a bit of luck and patience, visitors can catch a glimpse of these majestic creatures swimming in the waters surrounding Cameo Island.

We Return to Agios Sostis port.
